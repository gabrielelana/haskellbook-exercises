** State the ~Applicative~ laws                                                             :drill:
*** Answer
1. Identity
   #+BEGIN_SRC haskell :eval never
   pure id <*> v = v
   #+END_SRC
2. Composition
   #+BEGIN_SRC haskell :eval never
   pure (.) <*> u <*> v <*> w = u <*> (v <*> w)
   #+END_SRC
3. Homomorphism
   #+BEGIN_SRC haskell :eval never
   pure f <*> pure x = pure (f x)
   #+END_SRC
4. Interchange
   #+BEGIN_SRC haskell :eval never
   u <*> pure y = pure ($ y) <*> v
   #+END_SRC
